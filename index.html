About Me

I have been interested in how things work from an early age - indeed my parents thought they had a genius on their hands when they got up the morning after I received my first Rubik's cube to discover I had unscrambled it - until I told them that I had disassembled it with a screwdriver and put it back together again! My career in IT began some 30 years ago, starting in technical support (again figuring out how things work!) but moving into more data-related roles. Despite never having seen myself as a programmer, learning Python a couple of years ago sparked my desire to develop more technical skills, and so here I am, working my way through a degree in Data Science.

In my spare time (when I get any!) I enjoy playing board games and RPGs like Dungeons & Dragons, cross stitch, and spending time with my family (that's them in the photo above, although the kids have grown up a lot since then!)
